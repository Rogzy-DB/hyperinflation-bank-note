# Nicaragua Hyperinflation (1987-1990)

## Overview

Nicaragua experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1987 to 1990.
The Córdoba lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 33,548% annually.

## Causes

Civil war, US embargo, Sandinista policies

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1987
- **Peak**: 1988
- **End**: 1990

## Resolution

Currency reform, new Córdoba
