# Georgia Hyperinflation (1993-1994)

## Overview

Georgia experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1993 to 1994.
The Coupon lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 15,607% annually.

## Causes

Civil war, Russian gas cutoff
