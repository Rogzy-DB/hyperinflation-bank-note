# Lebanon Hyperinflation (2019-present)

## Overview

Lebanon experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 2019 to present.
The Pound lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 200%+ annually.

## Causes

Banking crisis, political dysfunction, Beirut explosion

## Timeline

- **Start**: 2019
- **Peak**: 2021-2023
- **End**: present
