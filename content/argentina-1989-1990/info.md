# Argentina Hyperinflation (1989-1990)

## Overview

Argentina experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1989 to 1990.
The Austral lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 3,079% annually.

## Causes

Debt crisis, fiscal deficit

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1989
- **Peak**: July 1989
- **End**: 1990

## Resolution

Convertibility Plan, Peso pegged to USD
