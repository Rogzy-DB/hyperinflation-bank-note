# Turkey Hyperinflation (1990-2005)

## Overview

Turkey experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1990 to 2005.
The Lira lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 105% annually average.

## Causes

Chronic fiscal deficits, political instability

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1990
- **Peak**: 2001 crisis peak
- **End**: 2005
