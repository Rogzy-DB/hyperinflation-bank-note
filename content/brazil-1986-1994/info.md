# Brazil Hyperinflation (1986-1994)

## Overview

Brazil experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1986 to 1994.
The Cruzado / Cruzeiro lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 2,477% annually.

## Causes

Debt crisis, political instability

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1986
- **Peak**: March 1990
- **End**: 1994

## Resolution

Real Plan, introduction of Real
