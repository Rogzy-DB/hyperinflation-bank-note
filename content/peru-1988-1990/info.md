# Peru Hyperinflation (1988-1990)

## Overview

Peru experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1988 to 1990.
The Inti lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 7,649% annually.

## Causes

Garcia government policies, debt default

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1988
- **Peak**: August 1990
- **End**: 1990

## Resolution

Fujishock reforms, Nuevo Sol
