# Greece Hyperinflation (1941-1944)

## Overview

Greece experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1941 to 1944.
The Drachma lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 8.55 billion % monthly.

## Causes

Nazi occupation, food requisition

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1941
- **Peak**: October 1944
- **End**: 1944

## Resolution

Liberation, currency reform
