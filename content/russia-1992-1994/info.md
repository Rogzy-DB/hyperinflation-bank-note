# Russia Hyperinflation (1992-1994)

## Overview

Russia experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1992 to 1994.
The Ruble lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 2,520% annually.

## Causes

Soviet collapse, price liberalization

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1992
- **Peak**: 1992
- **End**: 1994

## Resolution

Stabilization, 1998 redenomination
