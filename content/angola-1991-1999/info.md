# Angola Hyperinflation (1991-1999)

## Overview

Angola experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1991 to 1999.
The Kwanza lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 4,145% annually.

## Causes

Civil war, oil dependency

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1991
- **Peak**: 1996
- **End**: 1999

## Resolution

Kwanza Reajustado redenomination
