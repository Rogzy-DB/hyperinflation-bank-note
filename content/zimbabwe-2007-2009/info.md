# Zimbabwe Hyperinflation (2007-2009)

## Overview

Zimbabwe experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 2007 to 2009.
The Zimbabwean Dollar lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 79.6 billion % monthly.

## Causes

Land reform, money printing, economic mismanagement

## Timeline

- **Start**: 2007
- **Peak**: November 2008
- **End**: 2009

## Resolution

Currency abandoned, dollarization
