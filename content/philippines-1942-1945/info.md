# Philippines Hyperinflation (1942-1945)

## Overview

Philippines experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1942 to 1945.
The Japanese Occupation Peso lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching Severe (unmeasured).

## Causes

Japanese military occupation, forced currency

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1942
- **Peak**: 1945
- **End**: 1945

## Resolution

Liberation, return to Philippine Peso
