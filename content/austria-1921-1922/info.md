# Austria Hyperinflation (1921-1922)

## Overview

Austria experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1921 to 1922.
The Krone lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 1,426%.

## Causes

Post-WWI economic collapse

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1921
- **Peak**: August 1922
- **End**: 1922

## Resolution

League of Nations intervention, Schilling introduction
