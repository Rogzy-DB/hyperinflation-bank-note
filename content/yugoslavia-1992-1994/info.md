# Yugoslavia Hyperinflation (1992-1994)

## Overview

Yugoslavia experienced severe hyperinflation during the period of 1992 to 1994.
The Dinar lost significant value, with peak monthly inflation reaching 313 million % monthly.

## Causes

War, sanctions, economic collapse

## Timeline

- **Start**: 1992
- **Peak**: January 1994
- **End**: 1994

## Resolution

New Dinar pegged to Deutsche Mark
